- **First** ‚Äî Analyze why (pipeline/conversion/external factors)

- **Third** ‚Äî Seek coaching from top performers
- **Fourth** ‚Äî Communicate action plan transparently to manager
- **Fifth** ‚Äî Refine approach to prevent recurrence
