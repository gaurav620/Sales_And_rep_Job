## üé¨ AI Interview Success Tips

### Before Recording ‚úÖ

**Technical Setup:**

- Test camera and mic 30 minutes before
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Close all other apps/tabs
- Use headphones for better audio
- Charge your device fully

**Environment:**

- Clean, professional background (plain wall ideal)
- Good lighting (face the light, not window)
- Quiet room with no interruptions
- Sit at desk/table (not bed/couch)

**Personal Preparation:**

- Dress professionally (business casual minimum)
- Keep water nearby
- Have notes visible but don't read from them
- Practice answers out loud 3-5 times

### During Interview ‚úÖ

**Camera & Body Language:**

- Look directly at the camera (not the screen)
- Sit upright with good posture
- Smile naturally
- Use hand gestures (but not excessively)
- Position camera at eye level
- Maintain 70-80% eye contact with camera

**Speaking:**

- Speak clearly at moderate pace
- Avoid filler words (um, uh, like)
- Use confident tone
- Don't rush‚Äîtake 2-3 second pause before answering
- Emphasize key numbers (100-120 dials, 90% conversion, top 10)

**Content:**

- Answer in 30-45 seconds (don't ramble)
- Use the STAR method for behavioral questions
- Give specific examples with numbers
- Stay positive and enthusiastic
- End answers confidently (don't trail off)

### Don'ts ‚ùå

- ‚ùå Don't read from script (looks unnatural)
- ‚ùå Don't look away from camera frequently
- ‚ùå Don't fidget or touch your face
- ‚ùå Don't speak too fast (AI may not catch everything)
- ‚ùå Don't give vague answers without specifics
- ‚ùå Don't exceed time limits (answer gets cut off)
